Welcome to the Great Brook Run 2021

After a pandemic enforced pause, the Great Brook Run is back! It will take place on Tuesday 28th December 12.30pm at the Tite Inn, Chadlington (OX7 3NY).

For those of you unfamiliar with the event, the run starts and finishes at The Tite, and it’s a mile running through boggy fields and up a babbling brook. You will get wet!

As ever, fancy dress is encouraged and it’s a great occasion for runners and spectators alike.

Registration starts from 11am at The Tite and it is open to adults and children. Under 13s must be accompanied one to one by an adult and all entrants, including children, must register.

Entry fee – adult £10, under 18 £5, adult fancy dress £5. All money raised will be going to the Oxfordshire Association for the Blind, and every participant will get a medal.

PARKING – Please note that the pub car park will be closed and there will be no space outside the pub. If at all possible, come on foot. If you need to come by car, please park considerately in the village.
